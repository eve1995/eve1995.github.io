Salmon River Rafting

Your Salmon River Rafting rafting trip begins in the lower 100 miles of the canyon. Here, the Salmon River's famous rapids, though still exciting, are a bit tamer. The scenery is astounding. Massive sandstone cliffs conjure up imaginary shapes. Hidden waterfalls and pools carve their way down to the river. Wildlife is abundant, with frequent sightings of elusive Blue Heron, soaring hawks and eagles, and majestic Big Horn sheep. At night, the canyon walls unveil a heaven of innumerable stars. Come join us on an incredible and unforgettable Salmon River rafting journey.

Skill Level and Other Important Information

    Rapids: Level II

    Easy rapids, waves up to three feet tall that are readily seen, and wide channels that can be discovered without scouting. Some maneuvering is required during this little rock and roll.

    Rapids: III

    Easy rapids, waves up to three feet tall that are readily seen, and wide channels that can be discovered without scouting. Some maneuvering is required during this little rock and roll.

  • Raft Type: Western's J-Rig
  • Minimum Age: 9
  • Capacity: 18 - 28 passengers

Services Provided

  • MEALS: All meals are prepared for you between departure and return.
  • TRANSFERS: Bus from river to Company's parking lot.
  • EQUIPMENT: Rafts, paddles, lifejackets, helments, and wetsuits.

Starting at $1500
